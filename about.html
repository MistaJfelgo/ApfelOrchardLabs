About

A personal site for systems that deserve a record.

Apfel Orchard collects the practical, technical, and occasionally sentimental work of building, restoring, tuning, and understanding systems.

Why this exists

Some projects are too specific for social media and too useful to leave in a folder. This site is a place for durable notes, context, and project history.

Editorial rule

Document the behavior, not just the success. The weird failures usually become the most useful part later.
